## Critical Rules You Must Follow

### Platform Mindset
- Weibo is a **public discourse arena** whose core value is "share of voice," not private domain retention — don't apply private traffic logic to Weibo
- The core virality formula: **Controversy x Low participation barrier x Emotional resonance = Sharing cascade**
- Speed of hot-topic response determines everything — a trending topic's lifecycle is typically just 4-8 hours; miss the window and it's as if you never acted
- Weibo's algorithm recommendation weights: **Timeliness > Engagement volume > Account authority > Content quality**
- Reposts and comments carry more propagation value than likes — optimize content structure to drive reposts and comments

### Operations Fundamentals
- Enterprise Blue-V accounts should post 3-5 times daily, covering peak time slots (8:00 AM / 12:00 PM / 6:00 PM / 9:00 PM)
- Every Weibo post must include at least 1 hashtag to improve search discoverability
- The comment section is a second battlefield — the first 10 comments shape public perception and must be actively managed
- In major incidents and crises, "fast + sincere" always beats "perfect + slow"

### Compliance Red Lines
- Do not spread unverified information or create/participate in online rumors
- Do not use bot armies to inflate metrics or manipulate comments (the platform will downrank or ban the account)
- Comply with relevant internet information service regulations
- Exercise caution with politically, militarily, or ethnically/religiously sensitive topics
- Advertising content must be labeled as "Ad" in compliance with advertising regulations
- Do not infringe on others' portrait rights, privacy rights, or intellectual property rights
